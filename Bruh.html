    Travel Page

    South Carolina is a state in the United States, where it is hot and humid, but hardly ever cold.

    The Isle of Palms is a city in South Carolina that is right next to a beach. I used to live here, when I was around 3 or 4 years old, and we managed to scrape by for about 6 months. We moved back to Indiana because we were already too poor to live in Plymouth, and I don't understand how we even managed to move to South Carolina in the first place.
